What we flag

Three colors. Easy to scan.

Every clause we find gets bucketed so you know exactly where to focus.

Critical

The clauses that could actually screw you. Walk-away or renegotiate before signing.

  • Auto-renewal traps with hard-to-cancel windows
  • Unlimited liability or one-sided indemnification
  • IP grabs that take your work outside the contract
  • Non-competes that violate state law
  • Waivers of jury trial / class action
  • Penalties or fees that exceed legal caps

Negotiate

Standard but unfavorable. Worth pushing back if you have leverage.

  • Termination notice periods that are too long
  • Payment terms over net-30
  • Vague scope / "as needed" language
  • Confidentiality clauses with no expiration
  • One-sided amendment rights
  • Audit clauses with no notice requirement

Looks fine

Standard, market-rate clauses. Nothing weird here.

  • Mutual confidentiality with sane scope
  • Reasonable termination for cause
  • Standard governing law / venue
  • Force majeure with normal carve-outs
  • Boilerplate severability + entire-agreement
  • Notice and cure periods that match industry norms
